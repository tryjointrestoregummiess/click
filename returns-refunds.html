Shipping

CircadiYin can be shipped to most countries in the world, however, like other companies we are affected by the COVID19 pandemic.

Our goal with every order is to ship it out same day. If this is not possible, then we endeavour to ship your package as soon as physically possible. Recognised US holidays and postal holidays may affect your delivery time.

When your order is shipped, you’ll receive notification by email and you’ll receive a tracking link to locate and track the status of your order. If you have any questions about the shipping of your order, please reach out to our support team.

How Our Returns Work

If for any reason you are not totally happy with your purchase, you can notify us within 60 days of your order and arrange a return. To obtain a full refund, please return all bottles from your order, be they empty, partially full, or full. These must be sent back to our fulfillment center at the address listed below within 60 (sixty) days of your order date. If your entire order is not received and processed by our fulfillment center within 60 days of your order date, we will not issue your refund. In this event, any goods received will not be returned to you.

Along with your returned order, you must include full details relating to your order, including your order ID (found in your order receipt sent by email), your full name, your full address where your order was sent, your email address, and your phone number. These must all be clearly legible so our fulfillment center can identify your order and process your refund. Please reach out to our team if you are having any trouble with this process.

Once our fulfillment center has received all of the above, we will be able to process your refund. Your refund will be credited back to your chosen payment method within 5-10 business days. This also depends on the processing speed of your bank. If you do not meet these requirements we will be unable to process your refund. The customer must pay for the shipping to return products to us.
